Urban Glass Workshop

I would love to make a sparkly bead to add to my bracelets or make an earring from it. It’d be such a fun activity to do with your friends and exchange beads with each other for memory! This class in particular is $145.

#brooklyn #to-do

Nusprodukte lamp Workshop

I thought this workshop is really fun because the lamps you make are very unorthodox! It’s a bit stylish and seems like something you can’t find elsewhere. The class is $100-200.

#brooklyn #to-do

Brooklyn Glass Ring Workshop

A 3-hour glass ring class where you can expect to make around 3 rings! The class is about $215 and you need to book in advance. I think this would be such a great date idea and come back with friendship/couple accessories.

#brooklyn #to-do

SRC Risograph Workshop

This collaborative artist-run space and creative studio has independent publishing, art book production, and more! They usually have risograph classes a few times a month and we’d have to look at the website to see what kind of workshops are happening. They had a Pikachu riso print workshop awhile ago that I thought was quite cute. Classes range from around $60-$300 depending on the subject.

Maybe Happy Ending Broadway Show

Basically adding here so I can remember to watch with my boyfriend. He really wanted to watch this one, and I love broadway! This would be a fun, cute, romantic entertainment, I think.

#brooklyn #to-do

Lucky Risograph Workshop

This is a risograph printing press that works with local and international creators to reinterpret risograph with art books, zines, and projects. I LOVE the colours they have here. I feel like their workshops don’t happen often so we should try to catch one ASAP if we can! One class is around $200.
