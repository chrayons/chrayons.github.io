christina’s nyc bucket list

When I moved from Vancouver to New York City, I was amazed by how much there is to do. This site helps me collect the things I want to try and share them with friends, so we have something fun to do besides just dinner or a movie.

HOW TO READ THE CARDS:

  • Grey items = on my to-try list.
  • Blue items = been there, loved that.
    • If a blue card stays up, it’s a must-try!

HOW TO USE THE WEBSITE:

  1. Go to the page you’re interested in.
  2. Read the cards to help you choose where to go.
  3. Click on a card for more information.
